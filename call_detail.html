Coaching Summary
✅ What landed
1.Impact quantification at 12:40 — asking "what does that cost you?" and then using that number to close is the single most transferable technique on this call. Every rep should hear this sequence.
2.Silence after price at 30:02. Eight seconds of silence that the prospect broke. This takes discipline and most reps fill it. Marcus didn't.
3.The Isolate step on objection 3 — "if they're on board, are you ready to move forward today?" — confirmed the deal was there before offering the one-pager workaround. Eliminated the risk of a false objection killing the close.
🔧 One thing to sharpen
→Critical Event could have been established earlier — it appeared at 19:05, after the demo had already started. Surfacing the Q2 campaign deadline during discovery would have let Marcus frame the entire demo around that urgency from minute one.
💬 Quote of the call
"You mentioned this is costing you about $15,000 a month in wasted follow-up time. We're at $1,750. Even if we only solve half the problem, you're coming out significantly ahead. So the question isn't really about the price — it's about whether this is the right solution. And based on everything you've told me today, I think it is. What do you think?"
